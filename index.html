Sorter for local food to the wf house. Pick your sources, and hit the Start button.

Click on the character you like better from the two, or tie them if you like them equally or don't know them.



Keyboard controls during sorting: H/← (pick left)
J/↓ (undo)
K/↑ (tie)
L/→ (pick right)
S (save progress).

Before sorting: S/Enter (start sorting)
L (load progress).

1/2/3 always correspond to the first/second/third buttons.
